Welcome to a website all about Drake

Drake, born Aubrey Drake Graham on October 24, 1986, in Toronto, Canada, is a globally renowned rapper, singer, songwriter, and actor. He first gained recognition for his role as Jimmy Brooks on the television series "Degrassi: The Next Generation" before transitioning to a successful music career.
